# Elites POS — Features Jo Add Ho Sakte Hain

---

## Sabse Zaroori Features

### ✅ 1. Barcode Scanner Support
- Barcode scan karo aur product automatically cart mein aa jaye
- USB ya phone camera dono se kaam karega
- Backend mein `barcode` column already hai — sirf frontend mein thoda kaam baaki hai

### ✅ 2. Sales Return / Refund
- Customer product wapas kare toh refund process ho
- Stock automatically wapas add ho jaye
- Reports mein return history dikhe

### 3. Expense Tracking
- Roz ke kharche likho — bijli, kiraya, staff salary etc.
- Abhi profit sirf cost price se calculate ho raha hai, actual kharche nahi count ho rahe
- Yeh add hone se **sahi net profit** dikhega

### ✅ 4. Shift Management
- Cashier shift open kare aur opening cash enter kare
- Shift close karte waqt cash count kare
- End-of-shift report print ho
- Cash Out feature — shift ke doran drawer se cash nikalne ka record
- Shift delete (admin only — sirf closed shifts)
- Cash Out validation — available balance se zyada nahi nikal sakte

### 5. Discount / Coupon Codes
- Promo codes banao — flat ya percentage discount
- Checkout pe code apply karo
- Reports mein kitne coupons use hue woh dikhaye

### ✅ 6. Reports PDF / Excel mein Export
- Sales, inventory, P&L reports download ho saken
- Proper formatted Excel file with summary, tax column, ascending order

---

## Customer Waale Features

### 7. Loyalty Points System
- Har purchase pe points mile
- Agla purchase karte waqt points se discount lo
- Customer profile pe balance dikhe

### 8. Udhar / Credit Tracking
- Customer ne credit pe liya toh uski entry ho
- Har customer ka outstanding balance track ho
- Payment reminder bhi ho sake

### 9. Receipt Email / WhatsApp pe Bhejna
- Sale ke baad receipt customer ke email pe jaye
- Ya WhatsApp share link generate ho

---

## Inventory Waale Features

### 10. Supplier / Vendor Management
- Supplier ka naam, number, address save karo
- Purchase orders banao restock ke liye
- Pata rahe kaun si item kaun se supplier se aati hai

### 11. Product Variants
- Ek hi product ke alag sizes ya colors ho saken
- Jaise T-Shirt: Small, Medium, Large — har ek ka alag stock

### 12. Low Stock Notification
- Jab koi product threshold pe pahunche, alert aaye
- Dashboard bell pehle se hai — sirf real data connect karna hai

### ✅ 13. SKU Auto-Generate
- Category ke pehle 3 letters se SKU banta hai (BUR001, BIR001)
- Naya product add karte waqt automatically generate hota hai
- Admin manually bhi change kar sakta hai

---

## Advanced Features

### 14. Split Payment
- Ek sale mein aadha cash aadha card
- Jaise Rs. 500 cash + Rs. 300 card = Rs. 800 total

### 15. Multiple Branches
- Alag alag shop locations ka alag inventory aur sales
- Admin ko saari branches ka combined view mile

### 16. Custom Receipt Design
- Store logo lagao receipt pe
- Footer mein apna message likho
- Choose karo kya dikhana hai kya nahi

### 17. Sales Target / Goal
- Daily ya monthly target set karo
- Dashboard pe progress bar dikhe
